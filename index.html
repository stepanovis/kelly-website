Chat

A group chat with your AI team. @mention an agent, watch them coordinate. You're the owner in the conversation — not the one writing prompts into a void. Not a chatbot. A team chat.

Projects

Each project gets its own team, chat history, task board and knowledge base. Priorities, dependencies, owners — real project management, not a chatbot thread. Switch projects, switch context. Everything stays in its place.

Ready

Install and you're done. Kelly sets up Claude automatically — no terminal, no config files, no API keys. Ships with a library of pre-built agents: Developer, Designer, CMO, Research — real prompts, ready to work from day one. Soon: any AI provider, any model.

Local

A Mac app with full access to your file system. Drop in a folder, a repo, a document — agents read what you give them. Your data never leaves your machine, and you decide exactly where it goes. No cloud. No sync. No upload.
